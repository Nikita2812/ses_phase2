## 2. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The  purpose  of  the  meeting  was  to  present  TheLinkAI's  customized  AI  solution  system
designed to  completely transform and  automate  SES  engineering  operations.  This  initiative
positions  Shiva  Engineering  Services  to  become  the  first  company  to  execute  complete
engineering transformation in the EPCM domain through AI automation.
The  meeting  established  a  comprehensive  framework  for  transforming  all  engineering
operations across disciplines including Process, Mechanical, Electrical, Instrumentation, Civil
&  Structure,  Piping,  Fire  Fighting,  Project  Management,  Procurement,  and  Construction
Management.  The  solution  encompasses  three  core  modules:  Role-Specific  AI  Co-Pilots,
Workflow Orchestration, and Project-Specific Expertise.
Strategic  alignment  was  achieved  on  the  vision,  scope,  expected  outcomes,  and  12-month
implementation     roadmap.     The     partnership     approach     emphasizes     transparency,
collaboration, and shared success, with AI learning and improving iteratively through active
user participation and feedback.
This   transformative   journey   will   be  executed   over   12   months  with   clear   milestones,
governance  frameworks,  and  accountability  structures  to  ensure  successful  delivery  and
adoption across all engineering disciplines at SES.

## 6. PROPOSED AI SOLUTION - THREE CORE MODULES
The  Engineering  Automation  Copilot  comprises  three  integrated  modules  designed  to
transform operations comprehensively:
Module 1: Role-Specific AI Co-Pilots
- AI profiles created for each role level: HOD, Lead Engineer, Engineer, Designer
- Each  profile  based  on  experience  level,  responsibilities,  and  decision-making
authority
- AI  performs  primary  work  (prima  facie),  with  humans  conducting  review  and
validation
- Ensures appropriate level of automation and oversight for each organizational role
## Module 2: Workflow Orchestration
- Complete workflow for discipline-specific and project deliverables trained into AI
system
- Dynamic   workflow   adaptation   based   on   project   requirements   and   industry
standards
- Integration capabilities with third-party calculation tools and engineering software



## 6

- Automated handoffs between disciplines with built-in checks and balances
- SOP enforcement and compliance validation at each workflow stage
Module 3: Project-Specific Expertise
- AI  dynamically  trained  on  specific  project  technologies  (e.g.,  specialty  chemicals,
battery manufacturing, petrochemicals)
- Industry vertical knowledge applicable across all engineering disciplines
- Context-aware  recommendations  based  on  project-specific  requirements  and
constraints
- Continuous learning from project execution experience and user feedback

## 9. SECURITY & DATA MANAGEMENT FRAMEWORK
Security  is  paramount  in  the  system  architecture.  Five-pillar  security  approach  ensures
complete data protection:
## 9.1 Infrastructure Security:
- System  developed  and  deployed  on SES's  own  cloud  server (not  dependent  on
third-party AI service providers)
- End-to-end encryption for data at rest and in transit
- Private networking with no public internet exposure of sensitive data
- Isolated environment for AI model training and inference operations
9.2 LLM & Model Security:
- Open-source AI models deployed and trained in-house on SES's infrastructure
- No data sent to external AI service providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
- Complete control over model training data, algorithms, and outputs
- Ensures zero data leakage to external parties or competitors
## 9.3 Access Control:
- Strict role-based access control (RBAC) based on organizational hierarchy
- Project-level data isolation: Users only access data for their assigned projects and
roles
- Multi-factor authentication for system access and sensitive operations
- Principle of least privilege enforced throughout the system

## 9.4 Audit Trail & Standards Compliance:
- Complete  audit  trail: All  AI  interactions,  prompts,  and  outputs  are  logged  and
timestamped



## 9

- Full traceability of who accessed what data and when, with detailed activity logs
- System follows SES's standard SOPs and regulatory compliance requirements
- Version control for all generated documents and deliverables
- Support for regulatory compliance audits and quality management systems
## 9.5 Backup & Disaster Recovery:
- Three-tier  redundancy: Multiple  VPAs  (Virtual  Private  Appliances)  for  automatic
failover
- Two-layer backup strategy:
o GitHub for code versioning and configuration management
o AWS for weekly full system backups and data archival
- Disaster  recovery  procedures  with  defined  Recovery  Time  Objectives  (RTOs)  and
Recovery Point Objectives (RPOs)

## 15. CHANGE MANAGEMENT & REALISTIC EXPECTATIONS
15.1 AI Will NOT Be 100% Perfect on Day 1
It is critical to set realistic expectations: The Engineering Automation Copilot is designed to
learn  and  improve  with  usage.  An  initial  accuracy  baseline  will  be  established  for  Phase  1,
with continuous improvement driven by:
- Training on SES-specific data and past projects
- Fine-tuning based on user feedback and corrections
- Edge case handling as new scenarios are encountered
- Iterative model updates throughout the 12-month journey
15.2 Training & Fine-Tuning Process
- AI models improve iteratively with real engineering data, use cases, and feedback
- Weekly fixes and tuning during pilot phase (Months 3-6)
- Monthly stable releases with detailed release notes and version tracking
- Continuous feedback loop drives accuracy improvements over time
- Success requires partnership: users validate outputs and provide feedback
## 15.3 Issue Reporting & Continuous Improvement

using the issue reporting template
